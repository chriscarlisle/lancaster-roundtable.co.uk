Welcome to Lancaster Round Table

What is Round Table?

We are just a group of everyday lads aged between 18 and 45 who get together twice a month to try something new and have fun.

We do all sorts of different events and support local charities.

No funny handshakes, no entry criteria, just a group of guys looking to get out of the house and do more.
