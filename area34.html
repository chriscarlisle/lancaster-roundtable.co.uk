LANCASHIRE ROUND TABLES

AREA 34

Lancaster Round Table is so much more than just our club – we are part of ‘Area 34’ which covers most of Lancashire with other Tables in Lytham, Blackburn and Clitheroe.

As an area we support the work of each other’s clubs and meet up from time to time to compete for the coveted Area Sports Trophy or just to have a bigger, better social event. Luke from Lancaster Round Table is currently Area Chairman, so is our main link with the other clubs.
